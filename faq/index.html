    FAQ (Frequently Asked Questions)¶

    Do all roommates need accounts?¶

    Yes. Each roommate needs an account so chores and bills can be tracked fairly.

    Can I join multiple households?¶

    If your team limits to one household per account, then no. If your team supports multiple, explain how switching works.

    What happens if someone moves out?¶

    Admins can remove them. Bills and chore history remain for record-keeping.

    Can I edit or delete a chore/bill?¶

    Yes, if your app supports it. Typically the creator (or admin) can edit/delete.

    Does NARLA handle real money payments?¶

    Is my data private?¶

    Only household members should see household data. Passwords are never visible.

    What if someone refuses to mark chores complete?¶

    The system can show who is overdue. The best fix is household agreement + reminders.

    What devices are supported?¶

    Any modern browser. Mobile support depends on release platform.
