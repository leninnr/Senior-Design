# Getting Started

This page helps you start using NARLA quickly, even if you’re new.

---

## 1) Create Your Account
1. Open NARLA
2. Tap **Sign Up**
3. Enter your email and a secure password
4. Verify your email (if prompted)
5. Log in

> Tip: Use an email you actually check—NARLA may send invites and reminders.

---

## 2) Create or Join a Household
A **household** is your shared apartment group.

You can either:
- **Create** a household (if you’re the first roommate), or
- **Join** a household (if a roommate already created it)

---

## 3) Add Your Roommates
Once the household exists:
- Invite roommates by email **or**
- Share a join code / invite link

---

## 4) Do Your First “Real” Actions
Try these in order:
1. Add 2–3 chores (trash, dishes, vacuum)
2. Add a bill (internet, electricity)
3. Add 2–3 groceries (milk, eggs, rice)

After that, you’re basically fully set up.

---

## Common First-Day Questions
- “Do we all need accounts?” → Yes, each roommate needs an account.
- “Can we change who pays?” → Yes, bills can be edited later.
- “Can we turn off reminders?” → Yes, in settings.
