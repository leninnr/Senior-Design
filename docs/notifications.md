# Notifications

Notifications help your household stay on track.

---

## What NARLA Can Notify You About
- Upcoming chores
- Overdue chores
- Bills due soon
- Unpaid bills
- Groceries running low

---

## Managing Notification Settings
Go to:
**Settings → Notifications**

You may be able to toggle:
- Push notifications (phone)
- Email notifications
- Quiet hours (no alerts overnight)

---

## Recommended Settings
Most roommates like:
- Chore reminders: 12–24 hours before due
- Bill reminders: 3 days before due
- Low-stock alerts: enabled
