# Installation & Access

NARLA can be accessed as a web app (and may also have a mobile app release depending on your team’s final delivery).

---

## Requirements
- Internet connection
- A modern browser: Chrome, Safari, Firefox, Edge
- Phone or laptop

---

## Accessing NARLA
1. Open the NARLA URL provided by your team
2. Tap **Log In** or **Sign Up**

If your team publishes a mobile app later:
- Download from the App Store / Google Play
- Log in using the same account

---

## Troubleshooting Access
If the page won’t load:
- Confirm you’re connected to Wi-Fi
- Try a different browser
- Turn off ad blockers temporarily
- Check [Troubleshooting](troubleshooting.md)
