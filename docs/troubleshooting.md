# Troubleshooting

If something is not working, use this checklist.

---

## Login Problems
**Issue:** “I can’t log in.”
Try:
- Confirm email/password
- Reset password
- Check if your email needs verification
- Try a different browser

---

## Household Invite Problems
**Issue:** “The code doesn’t work.”
Try:
- Confirm you typed it correctly (no spaces)
- Ask for a fresh invite link
- Confirm you aren’t already in another household (if limited to one)

---

## Chores Not Updating
Try:
- Refresh the page
- Log out and log back in
- Check your internet connection

---

## Bills Math Looks Wrong
Try:
- Confirm number of roommates
- Confirm split type (equal vs custom)
- Confirm the total amount is correct

---

## Notifications Not Coming
Try:
- Turn on notifications in your phone settings
- Check NARLA notification settings
- Try email reminders if push is off

---

## Still Stuck?
Use the [FAQ](faq.md) or contact your team’s support method (email/issue tracker).
