# Chores

Chores keep everyone accountable and reduce arguments.

---

## Creating a Chore
1. Go to **Chores**
2. Tap **Add Chore**
3. Fill in:
   - Name (ex: “Take out trash”)
   - Assigned roommate
   - Due date or schedule (daily/weekly)
   - Notes (optional)
4. Tap **Save**


---

## Assigning Chores Fairly
Good practice:
- Rotate chores weekly
- Assign based on availability
- Keep chores small and clear

Example rotation:
- Week 1: Trash → Roommate A
- Week 2: Trash → Roommate B
- Week 3: Trash → Roommate C

---

## Marking a Chore Complete
1. Open the chore
2. Tap **Mark Complete**
3. Add optional comment (ex: “Done at 9pm”)

---

## Recurring Chores
Use recurring schedules for things like:
- Trash (weekly)
- Bathroom clean (weekly)
- Dishes (daily)

---

## Chore Reminders
Reminders can be:
- Push notifications
- Email notifications
- In-app alerts

You can adjust reminders in [Notifications](notifications.md).
