# Glossary

**Household**  
A shared group for one apartment/home. Roommates share chores, bills, groceries.

**Roommate / Member**  
A person inside your household.

**Admin**  
A household member with permission to manage members/settings (if supported).

**Chore**  
A task like trash, dishes, cleaning.

**Recurring chore**  
A chore that repeats on a schedule (daily/weekly).

**Bill**  
A shared expense like rent, utilities, internet.

**Split**  
How a bill is divided between roommates (equal/custom).

**Inventory**  
A shared list of items in the household (groceries/supplies).
