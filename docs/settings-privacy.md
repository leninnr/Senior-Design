# Settings & Privacy

This page explains account settings and what data is visible.

---

## Account Settings
In Settings you can typically:
- Change name and profile details
- Change password
- Update notification preferences

---

## Household Visibility
Household members may see:
- Your chore assignments and completion status
- Your bill payment status (paid/unpaid)
- Shared grocery updates

They should NOT see:
- Your private password
- Payment card details (if your app doesn’t store them)
- Other households (if multi-household is not supported)

---

## Privacy Notes (Simple)
NARLA is designed so that:
- Only household members can view household data
- You can leave a household if you move out
- Admins can manage membership

---
