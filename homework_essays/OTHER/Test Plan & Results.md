# 🧪 Test Plan and Results

## 🧠 Testing Approach
The NARLA application was tested through manual and functional testing by the development team. Each core feature was tested individually and as part of the full system to ensure correct behavior and usability.

Testing focused on:
- Core functionality (chores, bills, groceries, inventory)
- User flows (sign up, navigation, adding data)
- Real-time updates across the system
- UI responsiveness and usability

---

## ✅ Test Cases and Results

### 🔐 Authentication
- **Test:** User sign-up and login  
- **Result:** Users were able to successfully create accounts and log in without errors  

---

### 📊 Dashboard
- **Test:** Display of household data and activity  
- **Result:** Dashboard correctly displayed expenses, activity, and summary data  

---

### 🧹 Chore Management
- **Test:** Create, assign, and track chores  
- **Result:** Chores were successfully created, assigned, and updated in real time  

---

### 💳 Bill Splitting & OCR
- **Test:** Add bills and scan receipts  
- **Result:** Expenses were correctly recorded and split among users  
- OCR successfully extracted items from receipts for bill tracking  

---

### 📦 Inventory Management
- **Test:** Add, update, and remove items  
- **Result:** Inventory changes were reflected immediately and accurately  

---

### 🛒 Grocery List
- **Test:** Add and update grocery items  
- **Result:** Items were shared across users and updated correctly  

---

### 🤖 AI Insights
- **Test:** Generate household insights  
- **Result:** AI provided relevant suggestions based on available data  

---

## ⚠️ Limitations
- Testing was performed on a small group (developers only)  
- Limited real-world usage scenarios and edge cases  
- OCR accuracy may vary depending on receipt quality  

---

## 🔮 Future Testing

Future testing will involve larger groups of users in real shared living environments, such as:
- College apartments  
- Fraternities and sororities  
- Shared student housing  

This will allow:
- Testing at scale with multiple users  
- Evaluation of fairness in chore distribution  
- Improved validation of real-time synchronization  
- Better feedback on usability and user experience  

---

## 🧾 Summary
The testing process confirmed that NARLA’s core features function correctly and provide a reliable platform for managing shared living. Future large-scale testing will further improve robustness and usability.
